## Our Apps

### Golfiasta — GPS Golf Companion

Golfiasta is our golf utility app for iOS. It puts real course data in your pocket with no subscription required.

- **Find any course** — Search thousands of US golf courses by name or browse nearby. Full offline database means no cell signal needed on the course.
- **Satellite hole maps** — View every hole from above with fairways, greens, tees, and bunkers rendered on a satellite map.
- **Live GPS distances** — Get accurate front, center, and back distances to the green updated in real time as you move.

[Learn more about Golfiasta](golfiasta.html)

### DollyCam — Dolly Zoom Camera

DollyCam turns your iPhone into a one-person dolly zoom rig. The app tracks a face in real time and adjusts zoom as you walk, recreating the Vertigo effect made famous by Hitchcock — no rail, no crew, no post-production.

- **Real-time dolly zoom** — Face tracking drives automatic zoom adjustment as you walk toward or away from your subject.
- **Dual camera** — Record from front and back cameras simultaneously with PiP or split-screen layouts.
- **Pro video controls** — 4K, ProRes 422, 10 built-in LUTs, cinematic shutter lock, manual focus with peaking, and more.

[Learn more about DollyCam](dollycam.html)

### MakeLive — Video to Live Photo Converter

MakeLive turns any video into a Live Photo. Pick a video, trim it to the perfect moment, choose your thumbnail frame, and save it as a real Live Photo — complete with the long-press animation.

- **Precise trimming** — Trim up to 3 seconds with drag handles and an overview timeline for long videos.
- **Thumbnail control** — Pick the exact still frame for your Live Photo.
- **Import from anywhere** — Browse videos from external drives, SD cards, and folders.

[Learn more about MakeLive](makelive.html)
