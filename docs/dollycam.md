# DollyCam

## Dolly Zoom Camera — Cinematic Video, Dual Camera

DollyCam turns your iPhone into a one-person dolly zoom rig. The app tracks a face in real time and adjusts zoom as you walk toward or away from your subject, recreating the Vertigo effect made famous by Hitchcock — no rail, no crew, no post-production.

---

## Dolly Zoom

Tap a face, pick a direction, and press record. The app handles the rest:

- **Walk Away** — zoom increases as you move back, keeping the subject the same size while the background stretches
- **Walk Toward** — zoom decreases as you approach, compressing the background behind the subject
- Adjustable zoom response and intensity sliders let you dial in the exact look
- Noise rejection and frame smoothing eliminate jitter from handheld movement
- Monotonic ratchet prevents zoom reversal during recording for a clean, one-direction push

---

## Dual Camera (PiP)

Record from front and back cameras simultaneously:

- Floating picture-in-picture window you can drag anywhere on screen
- Drag to the top or bottom edge for a 50/50 split-screen layout
- Combined mode composites both feeds into a single video file
- Separate mode saves two synchronized files — one from each camera

---

## Cinematic Exposure Lock

Lock the shutter to a 180-degree shutter angle at 24 or 30 fps for true film-look motion blur:

- Auto ISO adapts to changing light while shutter speed stays fixed
- Live ISO readout on screen
- Low-light and overexposure warnings so you never clip

---

## Color Grading with LUTs

Apply .cube LUTs in real time — on the preview and baked into the recording:

- 10 built-in looks: Cinema Teal, Nostalgic, Monochrome, Vivid, Warm Fade, and more
- Import your own .cube files from any source
- Intensity slider blends from 0% to 100%
- GPU-accelerated Metal rendering pipeline

## Pro Video Controls

- 4K and 1080p resolution

- Auto, manual, and manual + focus peaking modes
