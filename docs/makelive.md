# MakeLive

## Video to Live Photo Converter

Turn any video into a Live Photo with MakeLive. Pick a video from your library, trim it down to the perfect moment, choose your thumbnail frame, and save it as a real Live Photo — complete with the long-press animation you know and love.

---

## Trim to the Perfect Moment

Select any video from your photo library and use precise drag handles to trim up to 3 seconds of footage. An overview timeline lets you quickly navigate through long videos so you never have to scrub frame by frame. Videos stored in iCloud start playing instantly — no waiting for downloads.

---

## Choose Your Still Frame

Pick the exact frame that becomes the still image for your Live Photo. Drag the keyframe marker through your trimmed clip to land on the perfect expression, the peak of the action, or any moment you want frozen in time.

---

## Preview Before You Save

See your Live Photo in action before committing. Long-press the preview to watch the animation play back exactly as it will appear in your Photos library, on your Lock Screen, or when shared.

---

## Import from Anywhere

Browse videos from external drives, SD cards, and folders — perfect for turning camera footage into Live Photos without importing to your photo library first. Your selected folder is remembered between sessions.

---

## Key Features

- Select any video from your photo library
- Trim up to 3 seconds with precise drag handles
- Overview timeline for quick navigation in long videos
- Pick the exact still frame for your Live Photo thumbnail
- Preview your Live Photo before saving
- Import from external drives, SD cards, and folders
- iCloud videos start playing instantly
- Step-by-step progress when creating Live Photos
- Saves as a proper Live Photo to your library
