How it works

Four taps to a perfect Live Photo.

1. Pick a video

Any video from your Photos library. iCloud videos start playing instantly — no waiting for downloads.

2. Trim precisely

Drag handles to trim up to 3 seconds (10s with Extended Trim). Overview timeline for long videos.

3. Pick the thumbnail

Drag the keyframe marker to the exact frame you want frozen as your still.

4. Save

Long-press to preview, then save as a proper Live Photo to your library — with the animation and everything.
