Golfiasta · iPhone & iPad · $6.99

Golf GPS, Scorecard
& Satellite Map.

Find any golf course, get live GPS distances, and track your scores — all on your phone. If you know how to use Apple Maps, you already know how to use Golfiasta. Just search, tap Play, and go.

Download on App Store See features
Find courses

Thousands of courses. Instantly.

Search by name or tap the location button to discover courses near you. Golfiasta includes a bundled database covering the US, Canada, and Mexico — so course data loads instantly without waiting for a server. International courses load online via OpenStreetMap.

Hundreds of previously unnamed courses now show their real names, matched from USGA's official course registry.

Live GPS

Front, center, and back — updated in real time.

Select a course and tap Play. Every hole is displayed on a satellite map with tee boxes, fairways, greens, and bunkers rendered as color-coded overlays. See live yardage to the front, center, and back of the green — calculated from actual green polygon geometry, not just pin distance.

Each hole view automatically aligns the camera to the tee-to-green bearing with a 45° tilt, so you see the hole the way you'd see it from the tee.

Shot planning

Plan your layup and approach — in one view.

Tap anywhere on the map to place a waypoint. Golfiasta instantly shows the distance from your position to the waypoint and from the waypoint to the pin, so you can plan layups, dogleg plays, or carry distances before you swing.

New: dual waypoints, fully draggable with real-time yardage updates as you move them.

Scorecard & history

A scorecard that feels like paper.

Tap any hole to enter your score with a simple +/- stepper anchored to par. Birdies get circles, bogeys get squares. Share your completed scorecard as an image when you finish.

Review past rounds with scores, par comparisons, and course details. Your round history syncs across your Apple devices via iCloud.

Everything included

Built for the course.

No accounts. No ads. No monthly fee. Just the features golfers actually use.
