    Golf GPS, Scorecard & Map

    Description (4000 chars max)

    Find any golf course, get live GPS distances, and track your scores — all on your phone. If you know how to use Apple Maps, you already know how to use Golfiasta. No tutorials, no sign-up screens, no learning curve. Just search, tap Play, and go. Satellite views of every hole, shot planning tools, front/center/back yardage to the green, and a built-in scorecard — no subscription required.

    FIND COURSES INSTANTLY Search by name or tap the location button to discover courses near you. Golfiasta includes a bundled database of thousands of US courses so course data loads instantly without waiting for a server, and can find international courses online via OpenStreetMap.

    PLAY MODE WITH LIVE GPS Select a course and tap Play — that's it. Every hole is overlaid on a satellite map with tee boxes, fairways, greens, and bunkers clearly marked. Swipe through holes with the picker at the bottom. Pinch, pan, and rotate the map just like you would in any maps app.

    GPS DISTANCES TO THE GREEN See live yardage to the front, center, and back of the green, updated continuously as you move. Distances are calculated from actual green polygon geometry when available, giving you accurate reads — not just pin distance.

    SHOT PLANNING Tap anywhere on the map to place a waypoint. Golfiasta instantly shows the distance from your position to the waypoint and from the waypoint to the pin, so you can plan layups, dogleg plays, or carry distances before you swing.

    HOLE-BY-HOLE NAVIGATION Each hole view automatically aligns the camera to the tee-to-green bearing with a 45-degree tilt, so you see the hole from the perspective you'd see it from the tee. Swipe through holes or tap "All" to see the full course layout. Auto-advance automatically moves you to the next hole when you finish putting and walk toward the next tee — no tapping required. Toggle it on or off anytime.

    DETAILED COURSE FEATURES Fairways, greens, tee boxes, and bunkers are rendered as color-coded overlays directly on the satellite map. Features are filtered per hole to keep the display clean and fast.

    SCORECARD & SCORE TRACKING Keep score as you play with a beautifully designed scorecard. Tap any hole to enter your score with a simple +/- stepper anchored to par. Birdies get circles, bogeys get squares — just like a real paper scorecard. Your scores are saved automatically when you finish your round.

    ROUND HISTORY Review all your past rounds with scores, par comparisons, and course details. Tap any saved round to see the full hole-by-hole breakdown, or hit Play Again to jump straight back into play mode on that course. Swipe to delete rounds you no longer need. Your round history syncs across your Apple devices via iCloud.

    NO SUBSCRIPTION. NO ADS. Golfiasta is a one-time purchase. No monthly fees, no ads, no account required. Your data stays on your device and syncs securely via iCloud.

    BUILT WITH OPENSTREETMAP DATA Course layouts are derived from OpenStreetMap (openstreetmap.org), available under the Open Database License (ODbL). Map data is contributed by a worldwide community of volunteer mappers. For license details, visit opendatacommons.org/licenses/odbl. GPS distances are approximate and should not be used as a substitute for official course markers or rangefinders.

    Screenshot Guidance

    App Store requires screenshots for at least 6.7" (iPhone 15 Pro Max / 16 Pro Max) and 6.1" displays.

    Recommended screenshots (in order): 1. Hole detail with GPS — Single hole view showing fairway/green/bunker features, the distance HUD (front/center/back), and the yardage line 2. Scorecard — The skeuomorphic scorecard overlay with scores entered for several holes, showing birdie/bogey decorations 3. Shot planning — Hole view with a waypoint placed, showing both distance segments with yardage labels 4. Course search results — Map showing multiple course boundaries with the course picker visible at the bottom 5. Round history — The round history list showing saved rounds with scores and relative-to-par 6. Search by name — The search bar with text entered and results shown

    Capture these on a real device or simulator at the correct resolution. No bezels or added text required — Apple displays them in device frames automatically.
